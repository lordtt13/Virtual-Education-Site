Design and test the operation of precision rectifier using Op-amp IC741

  
      

AIM:

i. To study and test the operation of half wave rectifier

ii. To study and test the operation of full wave rectifier

SOFTWARE/SIMULATION PLATFORM:

ORCAD AND TRISLAB

THEORY:

Full Wave Rectifier A full wave rectifier or absolute value circuit is shown in the circuit diagram foe positive input Vi > 0, diode D1 is on D2 is OFF. Both the op-amp A1 and A2 act as inverter for negative input ie Vi < 0 diode D1 is OFF and D2 is ON

PROCEDURE:

1. Connect the circuit as shown in the circuit diagram using Visual spic

2. Apply the sine waveform as the input to circuit with frequency 1KHz

3. Measure the frequency of oscillation and voltage through the oscilloscope
