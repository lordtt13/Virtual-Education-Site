Active filters

  
      

AIM:

To construct and plot the frequency response of the second order low pass filter and high pass filter. Measure the cut-off frequency in each case.

SOFTWARE/SIMULATION PLATFORM:

ORCAD AND TRISLABS

THEORY:

A frequency selective circuits that electric signal of specified band of frequencies are attenuates the signal frequency outside the band in an electic filter. At low frequency the passive filters have certain problems due to inductor. This is overcome in active filter where Op-amp and RC network is used.

Since an Op-amp is used the circuit offer high input impedance and low cost. becauuse of the large value of resistor can be used there by reducing the value of the capacitor. An improved response can be obtained by using a secong order active filter. A second order filter consists of two RC pairs and has roll off rated of 0dB/decade

Low pass filter: It is one that pass signal of frequencies below cut-off frequency and attenuates signal above the cut-off frequency.

High pass filter: It is one that pass signal of frequencies above cut-off frequency and attenuates signal below the cut-off frequency.
