Design a Triggering Switch using UJT

  
      

AIM:

Design the circuit for verification of UJT as Triggering Switch.

SOFTWARE REQUIRED:OrCAD/PSpice

Orcad/Capture CIS-->Analog Library-R, Source Library-Vdc, UJT-2N2222A/ZTX,Zener Diode-1N963B, Ground(GND)-0(zero)

Simulation Settings: Analysis Type-Time Domain(Transits) Point-40ms

Out Put Reading: Voltage Differential Marker(s)

THEORY:

If VEE <0.7V, Then no holes will go from P to N region as IE=0. If V1=0.7, it will act as forward bias and holes will go from P to n region and holes will move forwards B1&holes will be repelled by VBB to holes in B2 side ,so VB1E will decrese & IE will increase .
